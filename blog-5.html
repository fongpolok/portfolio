Ointment and Special Coating on Gasket

Background

This is a R&D project on developing design of tailor-made machinery and special coating material for ointment production line in Hong Kong that have inclonable ID when exposed in UV light.

At this moment, the client would like to product a medical oil based ointment product. Yet, ,edical oil will infiltrate and corrode the parts in the machinery during production results in failing to produce the product (Limited by current processing technology)

Especially plastic parts like gasket and tubing.

Machinery design

HKPC has to short list the right machinery for the client to produce their product, at the same time tailor made machinery parts to fit for their usage.

Upon manufacturing, it is of utmoust importance for to take care the tubing and channels that will contact the medical oil. The gasket material shall also be taken good care as well.

As shown in the right photo,the locations which are markted in red rectangles are the location which the gasket will touch the medical oil. Hence, during fabrication of machines this shall be noticed carefully.

Gasket Material

After confirming the design of the machinery, we then proceed to the part of gasket selection in material and coating technology. We need to first select the material that can withstand high tempature, as the whole emulsifying process shall be taken place in a high tempature tank.

We then need to conduct a corrsion test based on the medical oil. This can test out which material would be a better option in having a chemical resistance to the medical oil provide by the client.
