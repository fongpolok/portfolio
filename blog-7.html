MES System for medical oil production

Background

This is a R&D project on developing a Manufacturing Execution System (MES) for a Chinese Medicine Manufacturer in Hong Kong.

The client would like to have smartify thier machines, by obtaining the productivity and the unique ID of the products. This project can be divided into UI development and Vision setup.

UI/HMI Development

HKPC has provided consultancy service in developing a prototype UI/HMI for the MES system.

As shown in the photo, the sytem diplays the working order, together with the targeted numbers and current numbers of producted product. By a simple data visualization, the client can easily check on the current progress for production.

By providing this technology, the client traces back their products. They can also monitor the situation of the production

Vision and Feedback

Another part of the project is vision inspection, which a unique QR code will be embedded to the product packaging.

The QR will refer back to the types of proudcts, the expiry date of the products, the raw material of the products. As a result, this can help to trace back the production process if problems have been found in the end-product.

By implementing High Speed Camera, this can trace back the product products with back chekcing IDs so as to make sure each and every single products are being producted and logged in the system.
